North America Continuous Passive Motion (CPM) Machine Rental Market Drivers and Trends
The growing prevalence of orthopedic conditions and the increasing demand for post-surgery rehabilitation are key drivers for the North American CPM machine rental market. With a rising aging population and the need for effective recovery solutions, patients often seek CPM machines for faster and more comfortable healing. Moreover, hospitals, clinics, and home healthcare settings are increasingly relying on renting these devices due to their high costs, enabling broader access to advanced rehabilitation equipment. The growing awareness about the benefits of CPM therapy, such as reduced pain and improved mobility, further fuels this demand.

Another trend propelling the CPM machine rental market is the advancement of technology, leading to more compact, user-friendly, and affordable devices. These technological improvements enhance patient outcomes by providing customizable settings that cater to specific rehabilitation needs. As insurance providers increasingly cover CPM rentals, patients and healthcare providers are more inclined to adopt this cost-effective solution. Additionally, the increasing preference for home healthcare services is a significant trend, as patients opt for in-home therapy, minimizing hospital visits and enhancing convenience, thereby driving the growth of the rental market in North America.
