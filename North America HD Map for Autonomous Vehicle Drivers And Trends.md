HD Map stands for High Definition Map, it is a detailed and accurate map designed for autonomous vehicles to navigate and operate safely.

HD Map provides crucial data for autonomous vehicles such as lane markings, road signs, traffic signals, and obstacles, enabling safe navigation and decision-making.
