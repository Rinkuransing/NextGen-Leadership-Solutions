North America Kyphoplasty Balloons Market Drivers and Trends
The North America kyphoplasty balloons market is primarily driven by the rising prevalence of vertebral compression fractures (VCFs) due to aging populations and an increase in osteoporosis cases. As the elderly population grows, the demand for minimally invasive treatments like kyphoplasty is expected to rise.
